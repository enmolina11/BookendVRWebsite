In Bookend, play as a child who decides to read their favorite book, The Little Witch, while awaiting the apocalypse.

Harnessing the power of a child's imagination through VR, players assist the little witch Abigail
in her journey to save her village by intervening in critical parts of the story.

As the story progresses, both Abigail and the player come ever closer to their ends.






Platform(s): Oculus
Made in Unity
Release Date: June 2018
